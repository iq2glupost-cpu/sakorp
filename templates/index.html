FAQ

Common questions.

What exactly does SAKORP Shield monitor? +

Shield monitors your Amazon product listings on a regular scan cadence. It observes Buy Box ownership, seller presence, and listing status changes — logging every scan with a precise timestamp into your forensic timeline.

How is this different from checking manually? +

Manual checks are inconsistent, undocumented, and easy to miss. Shield runs automatically on a stable cadence, captures short-lived changes you'd never see manually, and creates a timestamped forensic log you can reference or export.

What's the difference between Standard and Protected ASINs? +

Standard ASINs are scanned approximately every 60 minutes. Protected ASINs receive ~30-minute scans plus AI-assisted signal context via Nexus — designed for your highest-revenue listings.

Is SAKORP Shield affiliated with Amazon? +

No. SAKORP Shield is an independent tool, not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Amazon. All monitoring outputs are observational and informational only.
